Our Beliefs

If you declare with your mouth, “Jesus is Lord,” and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved. For it is with your heart that you believe and are justified, and it is with your mouth that you profess your faith and are saved.

Romans 10:9-10


  • We believe that both the Old and New Testaments of the Bible are complete and reliable, inspired by God, and that they are the highest authority and rule of our lives.
  • We believe in the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, and that God is the creator of all things and the one true God.
  • We believe that Jesus is the only begotten Son of God, born of the Holy Spirit, and born of the Virgin Mary.
  • We believe that Jesus was crucified for our sins, that he died and resurrected three days after, that he ascended to heaven and sat on the right side of the throne of the highest.
